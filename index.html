What is WebMonitoring?

WebMonitoring is a client-side monitoring tool that checks the status of your websites via an API proxy. It helps developers and site owners ensure their services are online.

How it Works

1. Click Get Started to enter the dashboard.
2. Enter your website URL (e.g., example.com).
3. The system sends a request every 60 seconds.
4. If the status code is not 200 (OK), you will receive an alert.

Privacy

Your data is stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage). We do not store your monitored URLs on our servers.
